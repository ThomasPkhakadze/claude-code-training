Action Items

Recommendations

TechFlow saw exceptional growth in February 2026 -- sessions up 69.8% YoY, signups up 137%, and revenue up 154.5%. The Free Trial campaigns on Meta and Brand Search on Google are the top performers. CPA dropped 25.6% YoY to $12.62, showing strong efficiency gains. Focus in March should be on scaling winners and improving trial-to-paid conversion.

High Impact
Meta

Double Down on Free Trial Campaign

The Free Trial Promo has the highest CTR (4.2%) and best conversion rate on Meta. Increase budget by 30% and test new creative variations.

High Impact
Google

Expand Brand Search Budget

Brand search converts at 9.2% CTR with the lowest CPC. There is likely untapped search volume -- increase budget to capture all branded queries.

Medium Impact
Google

Optimize Competitor Comparison Campaigns

CTR is 5.0% but conversion cost is high relative to other campaigns. Review keyword targeting and landing page relevance.

Medium Impact
Website

Reduce Bounce Rate Further

Bounce rate improved from 44.6% to 38.2% YoY. Continue A/B testing landing pages, especially for paid traffic sources where first impressions are critical.

High Impact
Product

Improve Trial-to-Paid Conversion

Only 22% of active trials convert to paid. Implement an in-app onboarding sequence and targeted email nurture for trial users in week 2.
