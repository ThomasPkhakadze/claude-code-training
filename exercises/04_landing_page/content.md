## Features

### Feature 1: Adaptive Learning
ThermoSmart Pro studies your daily routine for the first week, then automatically adjusts temperatures based on when you wake up, leave home, and go to sleep. No manual scheduling needed.

**Icon idea:** Brain / lightbulb

### Feature 2: Weather-Aware
Connects to local weather data and pre-adjusts your heating or cooling before temperature swings hit. If a cold front is coming tonight, your home is already warming up.

**Icon idea:** Cloud with sun

### Feature 3: Room-by-Room Control
Pair with ThermoSmart sensors (sold separately) to control each room independently. No more heating the guest room nobody uses or freezing in your home office.

**Icon idea:** Grid / rooms layout

### Feature 4: Energy Dashboard
Track your energy usage in real time through the ThermoSmart app. See daily, weekly, and monthly reports with clear breakdowns of where your energy goes and how much you're saving.

**Icon idea:** Chart / graph
